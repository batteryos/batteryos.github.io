### Web - Font Sizes

- **Headings (h1 to h6):**
  - h1: 2.5rem
  - h2: 2rem
  - h3: 1.75rem
  - h4: 1.5rem
  - h5: 1.25rem
  - h6: 1rem
- **Body text**: 1rem (16px)

### Corner Rounding

**4px** rounding to be used universally.

## Do's and Don't's

**Do**: Ensure the logo and colors are used consistently across all mediums.

**Don't**: Stretch or distort the logo, and avoid using colors outside of the specified palette.
